See https://github.com/mongodb-developer/mongodb-search-java-server for details.
Main entry point: /search
Examples:
  • q=blues brothers&search=title
  • q=purple rain&limit=5&skip=0&project=title,genres,plot,year&search=title,plot&filter=genres:Music
  • facet string years across entire collection (requires year be indexed as `token`)
  • facet on string and numeric years (requires year be indexed as `token` and `number`)
